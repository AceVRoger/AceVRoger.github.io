Shape Area and Volume Calculator

This page is dedicated to finding the Perimeter, Area and Volume of circles as well as spheres

Circles


The Perimeter of a circle is also known as the circumference, which is the total distance covered by the boundary of the circle. which can be calculated by using this formula... P = 2 * PI * r.


The Area of a circle is the region occupied by the circle in a two-dimensional plane which is usually measured in squared units. Which can be calculated by using this formula... A = PI * r^2


Spheres


The Volume of a sphere indicates how big/ much space the sphere takes. which can be calculated by using this formula... V = 4/3 * PI * r^3
